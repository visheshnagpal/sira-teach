# Subject Guide Format

Use this format when adding a shareable subject guide to Sira. Subject guides distill source review into original guidance that can travel with the skill. Do not include private archives, child records, or long copied passages.

Name guides as `<SUBJECT>-GUIDE.md`, matching the subject folder name when possible.

## Required Shape

```md
# {Subject} Guide

This is Sira's shareable {subject} guide. It distills {subject} practices into original guidance that can travel with the skill. Do not include private archives, child records, or long copied source passages.

{What this subject does for a child, beyond school coverage.}
```

## Rules

- Keep the guide useful without private archive access.
- Keep the subject distinct even when it connects naturally to other subjects.
- Include pre-elementary ramps wherever the source material supports them.
- Include a K-12 horizon so today's elementary work knows what it is preparing for.
- Keep resources as a canon of leads, not assumed family possessions.
- Prefer parent-runnable practices over abstract philosophy.
- Include old-friends examples, but do not turn every subject into flashcards.
